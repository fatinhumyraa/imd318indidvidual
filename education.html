Middle School

At Sekolah Bukit Sentosa, I was surrounded by dedicated teachers and caring classmates who made learning enjoyable and meaningful. The school provided a supportive environment where I was encouraged to explore my interests, ask questions, and grow both academically and personally. From basic subjects like mathematics, science, and language to extracurricular activities like sports and arts, the school offered a well-rounded education that helped me discover my strengths.

High School

The values of discipline, respect, and teamwork that I learned at Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Bukit Sentosa 2 have stayed with me throughout my educational journey. It was here that I first realized the importance of hard work, curiosity, and a positive attitude towards learning, which has guided me in all my subsequent studies. Looking back, my time at Sekolah Bukit Sentosa was more than just an academic experience—it was where I started to build the character and mindset that continues to influence my life today.

University

Pursuing a Diploma in Information Management has been a transformative journey that has equipped me with the knowledge and skills necessary to thrive in today’s data-driven world. This program, offered by Universiti Teknologi Mara, has provided me with a strong foundation in the principles and practices of managing information in various organizational settings.
